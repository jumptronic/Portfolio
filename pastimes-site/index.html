What it does

Log It!

Log anything — oil changes, gym sessions, medication doses, date nights, haircuts. Pastimes answers the one question you're always asking yourself: when did I last do this? No social graphs. No streaks. No shame. Just an honest record of your life.

📖

A log, not a leash

Pastimes doesn't judge a gap in your activity. It just tells you the truth about when you last showed up.

🎯

Goals without guilt

Set a monthly target for things that matter — in-office days, workouts, calls home. Progress shown simply. No red bars.

✍️

Notes that stick around

Every entry can carry a note. The most recent one surfaces right at the top — your own words, right where you need them.

Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

📅

Quick logging

Tap once to log today. Tap a past date to backfill. Add a note, or don't — either way takes seconds.

📆

Visual calendar

A month view dots your logged days. Instantly see your pattern — or your honest gaps. Empty is fine. Empty is real.

🎯

Optional goals

Attach a monthly target to an activity if you want to track against something. The goal block only appears when you've set one.

🏷️

Categories & colors

Group activities by what makes sense to you. Pick a color. Make it yours. This is your diary, not ours.

⭐️

Favorites

Pin the activities you return to most. They're right there at the top, not buried three taps deep.

🔍

Search

Find anything instantly. Search by name, category, or note content. Your history, fully searchable.

🗓️

Multi-date logging

Log a run of days at once — select multiple dates on the calendar, write one note, and you're done.

The idea behind it

"It's more like a diary
than a scoreboard."

Most tracking apps want you to perform. Pastimes just wants to remember.

No streaks to break. No badges to earn. No friends to beat.
Just a quiet record of the things you care about — and when you last did them.
